How does Integrity Landscape Design look after your Needs?

The first step will be to meet with the prospective client at their property to discuss their landscaping requirements and obtain information relating to the property dimensions and the clients landscaping ideas and specific needs.

The second step the information gathered above would be transferred onto a hand drawn design sketch for the client to review prior to providing a detailed drawing with a planting plan. This drawing would then be reviewed with the client and alterations to the design, if required, will be made.

Finally when the client is fully satisfied with the design the designer will arrange on the clients behalf to obtain a landscaping quotation. On acceptance of the landscaping quotation, the designer is able to contract the landscaper on behalf of the client to execute the design plan & over see the landscaping project throughout the different stages. There will be an initial design ideas & consultation fee. The price will vary dependant on the size of the property. The final drawing, planting plan will be a separate charge.

I have run a landscaping business for over ten years & with landscaping in excess of fifteen years. Based on this experience I will ensure that the landscaping stages are completed to a professional level.

Specialising in:

  1. Landscaping & Garden Design
  2. Bluestone & Slate Landscaping
  3. Retaining Walls / Dry stone walls
  4. Paving: Steps & Pathways
  5. Real & Synthetic Turf
  6. Sustainable Native Planting
  7. Irrigation & Drip Feed Systems

Landscaping your property is the absolute best way to improve the “curb appeal” of your home. Whether you’re looking to improve the property value, or you just want to make your lawn & gardens look nice, it sure isn’t easy. It’s tough enough coming up with inspiration for the look you desire that will work with the challenges of the land you’re working with. What makes it more difficult is the up-keep and knowledge that must go along with it.
